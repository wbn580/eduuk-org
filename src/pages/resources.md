A curated index of authoritative UK study resources from government, universities, and independent research bodies. All links point to live, English-language pages.

## Government & Official Bodies

### Visas & Immigration
- **UKVI** — https://www.gov.uk/visas-immigration — UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) official guidance on all visa types
- **Student Visa: Fees & Duration** — https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/fees-payment — Current visa application fees and IHS surcharge
- **Graduate Route** — https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa — Post-study work visa for graduates

### Education & Quality
- **Office for Students** — https://www.officeforstudents.org.uk — Independent regulator of higher education in England
- **UKCISA** — https://www.ukcisa.org.uk — UK Council for International Student Affairs (student support, funding, rights)
- **HESA** — https://www.hesa.ac.uk — Higher Education Statistics Agency (official UK university data)

### Health & Essential Services
- **NHS** — https://www.nhs.uk — National Health Service; international students must register with a GP
- **UK Tax (HMRC)** — https://www.gov.uk/guidance/check-if-you-need-a-national-insurance-number — National Insurance (NI) number eligibility and application

## University Applications & Selection

### Central Application Systems
- **UCAS** — https://www.ucas.com — Central admissions for undergraduate and some postgraduate degrees
- **UCAS Tariff** — https://www.ucas.com/providers/registrants/tariff — A-level / IB / international qualification equivalencies

### University Groups & Associations
- **Russell Group** — https://www.russellgroup.ac.uk — 24 leading research universities (list of members)
- **G5** — Not an official acronym; refers to five top universities: Cambridge, Oxford, Imperial College London, LSE, UCL (no official registry)
- **Complete University Guide** — https://www.thecompleteuniversityguide.co.uk — Independent UK university rankings and profiles
- **QS World University Rankings** — https://www.topuniversities.com/world-university-rankings — Global university ranking (UK subset available)
- **Times Higher Education Rankings** — https://www.timeshighereducation.com/world-university-rankings — Alternative global ranking

### University Admissions Pages
Most universities maintain separate undergraduate and postgraduate admissions portals. Search "[University Name] admissions" for official links.

## Course & Subject Information

### Subject-Specific
- **The Guardian University Guide** — https://www.theguardianuniversityguide.co.uk — UK university subject league tables
- **HESA Subject Classifications** — https://www.hesa.ac.uk/filedepot_download/public/1 — Detailed course code and subject hierarchies

## Language Requirements

### IELTS
- **IELTS Official** — https://www.ielts.org — International English Language Testing System (most UK universities accept IELTS)
- **British Council IELTS** — https://www.britishcouncil.org/exam/ielts — UK-based test administration and registration

### Other English Tests
- **TOEFL** — https://www.ets.org/toefl — Accepted by many UK universities
- **Cambridge English Qualifications** — https://www.cambridgeenglish.org — CPE / CAE also accepted as proof of proficiency

## Accommodation

### University Housing
Most universities provide halls of residence information in their student accommodation pages (search "[University] halls" or "residence").

### Private Student Housing
- **UniHoz** — https://www.unihoz.com — Student property finder
- **SpareRoom** — https://www.spareroom.co.uk — Private flat rentals (UK-wide)
- **Rightmove** — https://www.rightmove.co.uk — General property rentals (includes student areas)
- **Deposit Protection Service (DPS)** — https://www.depositprotection.com — Tenancy deposit insurance scheme
- **TDS (The Deposit Solution)** — https://www.tds.gb.com — Another UK tenancy deposit protection scheme

### Tenant Rights
- **Shelter** — https://england.shelter.org.uk — Housing rights, deposit, and eviction guidance
- **Citizens Advice** — https://www.citizensadvice.org.uk/about-us/our-work/policy/policy-research-topics/housing-policy-research-and-consultation/ — Consumer rights and housing

## Finance & Cost of Living

### Student Finance & Loans (England)
- **Student Finance** (English students) — https://www.gov.uk/student-finance — Maintenance grants and loans for English domiciled students
- **Student Loan Company** — https://www.slc.co.uk — Loan administration and repayment

### Banking
- **Money Helper** — https://www.moneyhelper.org.uk — UK government financial guidance
- **Monzo** (app-based) — https://monzo.com — Popular option for international students (no address required at signup)
- **HSBC UK** — https://www.hsbc.co.uk/student/ — Major bank; international student accounts available
- **Lloyds Bank** — https://www.lloydsbank.com/student/ — Student account packages

### Cost of Living References
- **Save the Student** — https://www.savethestudent.org — Living costs, budgeting, part-time work rules
- **British Council** — https://www.britishcouncil.org/study-uk/living-costs — Annual cost estimates by location

## Transport & Travel

### Rail & Public Transport
- **16-25 Railcard** — https://www.railcard.co.uk/16-25-railcard — Discounted train travel (available to international students aged 16–25)
- **Tranport for London (TfL)** — https://tfl.gov.uk — London Underground, buses, Oyster cards
- **National Express** — https://www.nationalexpress.com — Long-distance coach service
- **Greyhound Coach UK** — https://coachuk.com — Budget coach operator

### Airport & International
- **Heathrow** — https://www.heathrow.com — Major London airport
- **Gatwick** — https://www.gatwickairport.com — South London alternative
- **Manchester Airport** — https://www.manchesterairport.com — Northern England hub

## Work & Employment

### Part-Time Work Rules
- **UKVI Student Work Conditions** — https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/working-hours-conditions — Hours and sector restrictions for international students
- **Universal Jobmatch** — https://www.gov.uk/jobsearch — UK job board

### Tax & National Insurance
- **HMRC Self-Assessment** — https://www.gov.uk/self-employed-national-insurance-rates — If you're self-employed or freelance

## Scholarships & Financial Aid

### UK-Wide Schemes
- **UKRI Doctoral Loans** — https://www.ukri.org/funding/how-to-apply/how-to-find-funding/ — UK Research and Innovation funding database
- **Chevening Scholarships** — https://www.chevening.org — UK government scholarship for international postgraduates
- **British Council Scholarships** — https://www.britishcouncil.org/study-uk/scholarships-funding — Extensive listing

### University-Specific
Most universities publish scholarships on their dedicated "Scholarships" or "Funding" page.
