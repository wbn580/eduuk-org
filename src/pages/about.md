**Study in UK** is a practical knowledge base for international students planning to study in the United Kingdom. We break down the most common questions — from G5 / Russell Group selection, degree paths, Student Visa, accommodation and daily life — into clear Q&A format: question = title, answer = content. All information is grounded in official UK government data (UKVI, UCAS, HESA) and real student experience. Factual, regularly updated.

This is **not** a study abroad consultancy. We don't accept commission from universities, make guaranteed admission promises, or offer paid consulting. All content is independent and fact-checked only.

## Our Editorial Position

- **Independence**: Zero paid partnerships with universities or ranked listings. Content is unbiased.
- **Timeliness**: UK education policy changes annually — Student Visa financial requirements, Graduate Route eligibility, tuition caps, IELTS waivers. We date every article and update major policy shifts within 48 hours.
- **Verifiable**: Numbers (fees, visa costs, IHS surcharge, scholarships) link back to UKVI, UKCISA, HESA, UCAS, or official university admissions pages.

## Five Content Pillars

| Pillar | Coverage |
|---|---|
| **Universities** | G5, Russell Group (24 schools), QS rankings explained, tiered selection strategy, China Accept List, transfers and Clearing |
| **Courses** | Business (finance / accounting / management), STEM (CS / AI / civil eng), Medicine, Law, Media, Education, Design |
| **Visa** | Student Visa (Tier 4), ATAS, CAS, BRP → eVisa, Graduate Route, Skilled Worker, dependant visas |
| **Accommodation** | University halls vs. private rental, deposit protection (DPS/TDS), agency fees, contract pitfalls, Council Tax exemption |
| **Life** | Bank accounts (Monzo/HSBC/Lloyds), NHS GP registration, transport (16–25 Railcard), 20-hour work limits, NI number |

Each pillar includes 12+ in-depth guides covering "before arrival" through "first 6 months" decisions.

## What We Don't Do

- **Avoid intermediary fees** — No integration with recruitment agents, no referral commissions
- **No anxiety-mongering** — No "last chance" or "act now" tactics; only timelines and options
- **No made-up data** — All rankings, acceptance rates, and fees cite official sources and academic year

## Information Hierarchy

1. **UK Government & statutory bodies**: UKVI (gov.uk/visas-immigration), UKCISA, HESA, Office for Students
2. **Official education portals**: UCAS, university Postgraduate/Undergraduate Admissions pages
3. **Tuition data**: HESA Student Fees datasets, university "International Tuition Fees" pages
4. **Visa fees**: gov.uk "Student Visa: Fees" and "IHS surcharge" latest pages
5. **Cross-check**: Save the Student, TopUniversities, The Complete University Guide
