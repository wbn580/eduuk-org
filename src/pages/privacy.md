*Last updated: April 2026*

**Study in UK** (website: `eduuk.org`) respects every visitor's privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in plain language.

## One-Sentence Summary

We operate a **purely static information site** — no account registration, no personal data collection (names, phones, addresses). We don't track you for advertising.

## What We Do **NOT** Do

- Require account creation or login
- Collect names, phone numbers, IDs, passports, or personal details
- Place ad tracking pixels (Google Ads / Facebook Pixel / similar)
- Sell visitor data to third parties

## What We **MAY** Collect

### Server Logs

We host on **Cloudflare Pages**. Cloudflare automatically records **anonymous technical data** for security and performance:

- Access timestamp
- IP address (used only for DDoS defense and geographic analytics)
- Browser type and version
- Page URL accessed

Cloudflare stores this data; we don't see raw logs. It's only used for attack mitigation and aggregate reporting.  
See [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

### Analytics (if enabled)

If we add **Cloudflare Web Analytics** in future, it is:
- **Cookie-free**
- **No personal ID tracking**
- Counts pageviews and traffic source (country only)
- **No** third-party cookies, no user behavior tracking

We explicitly **do not use** Google Analytics, Baidu Analytics, CNZZ, or similar tracking tools.

## Cookies

We **do not set tracking cookies**. Light/dark mode toggle and reading progress are stored in your browser's `localStorage` — **never sent to any server**.

## Third-Party Links

Articles link to official UK websites (university pages, GOV.UK, UCAS, HESA, etc.). Once you leave, those sites' privacy policies apply. **We are not responsible** for their practices.

## Minors

Our content targets students and families considering UK study. If you're under 18, browse with parental/guardian guidance. We don't deliberately collect minor information.

## Your Rights

Because we don't hold your personal data, there's no "request, update, delete account" process. Have questions or concerns about our privacy practices? Email us via the footer contact link.

## Policy Changes

We update this notice if our operations or features change. Updates appear in the "Last updated" date. Major changes appear on the homepage for ≥7 days.

## Contact

Privacy questions? Email the footer contact address.
